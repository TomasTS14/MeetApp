What is MeetApp?

An app for searching, managing, and creating events near you

MeetApp is an application for searching, managing, and creating events near you. With personalized event recommendations based on your interests and preferences, you can easily find relevant and meaningful events in your local area. The built-in social network also allows you to connect with other attendees before, during, and after the event.

MeetApp is the ultimate solution for event-goers and organizers alike. Whether you're looking for something to do on a weekend or organizing a large-scale conference, MeetApp's user-friendly interface and powerful features make it easy to discover and manage events in your local area.

  • Check events in your area and participate in them.
  • Create events and spread the word through the app.
  • Interact with other partakers and organizers alike.
